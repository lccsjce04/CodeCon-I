Simple Encryption program.


Input: String s

Output:String e (Encrypted String of s)

The rules for Encryption are as follows:

i)Convert all the consonants in s: 
	-> To its next consonant( Alphabetically )
ii)Convert all the vowels in s:
	-> To its number equivalent. 
	(a=1, b=2, c=3, d=4, e=5)
iii)Convert all the digits in s:
	->To its alphabet equivalent.
	(1=a, b=2 ... i=9, j=0)
iv)Space to *
